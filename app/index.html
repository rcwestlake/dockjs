This REPL is your home-base for learning Javascript. You can liken it to a good cup of coffee--something you return to everyday.

Transpile your Vanilla JS into ES6 or take your ES6 and transform it back to your favorite flavor. We have set up some alternatives to using 'console.log(vanilla ice cream)'; You may use 'pour', 'serve', 'brew', 'run', 'print', 'log', 'l', i.e "brew('a hot mocha breve')".

Enjoy your daily ritual.
